Logos

Logo Usage

Sizing

The logo is spatially related based off of its x-height value. In print publications smaller than legal size (17" x 11"), this x value should not exceed 2 inches and should not be smaller than 1/10 of an inch.

The logo may be larger in case of use on exterior signs and billboards.

Spacing

Direction

Direction:

Always face the logo upwards, and so that the text reads from left to right. Do not turn the logo horizontally or angle it. The logo must lie flat on a horizontal plane.

Coloring

Background Color:

Strawberry Red is always used as the background color behind the logo. Never use another color. White is acceptable only if the logo is on a white plane. If color is used behind the logo in a square, it must be the specific shade of Strawberry Red outlined in this style guide.

General Color:

The text color and colors of the actual fruit images must be the original colors chosen for them.
